About Me

A bit about my life

I'm a 23 year old with a keen passion for software development. I completed my VCE at Melbourne High School in 2015 and then went on to attend The University of Melbourne where I studied a Bachelor's in Science, majoring in Microbiology and Immunology. Though, I throoughly enjoyed the content presented in my Bachelor's degree, I grew to realise that a career as a researcher simply wasn't for me. Thus, began my exploration to find a career which would provide me with a true sense of fulfillment.

It was when I was tasked with designing a website for a family buinessnes, that I was initially introduced to the world of software development. From my initial forays into the field of web-development, I developed a passion for software development in a more holistic sense and, form there, I fell far and fell rather quickly down the cliched rabbit hole. Fast forward a few years, and here I am: knees-deep in the wide world of software development and as enthusiastic as ever.
