A bit about my life

Though, I throoughly enjoyed the content presented in my Bachelor's degree, I grew to realise that a career as a researcher simply wasn't for me. Thus, began my exploration to find a career which would provide me with a true sense of fulfillment.

It was when I was tasked with designing a website for a family buinessnes, that I was initially introduced to the world of software development. From my initial forays into the field of web-development, I developed a passion for software development in a more holistic sense and, form there, I fell far and fell rather quickly down the cliched rabbit hole.

From that point onward, I began online introductory CS courses and began teaching myself the ins-and-outs of everything programming. From C, Java and Python, through to data structures and algorithms, myt curiosity propelled me through myriad courses and textbooks until I finally felt I was ready to take my first big step and start looking for a career as a developer.
